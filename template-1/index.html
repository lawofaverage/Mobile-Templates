You can go back to the home page (or whatever internal page you arrived here from) by clicking the back button

If you refresh the browser, the back button will go away, leaving only the home button as a navigation choice.

The back button is setup to always take you back to where you just came from within the site.

If you refresh the browser and then click on the home button, the back button will show up on the Home page.
